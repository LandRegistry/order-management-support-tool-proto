Coded design prototype

What is the purpose of this prototype?

This is a coded version of the Business Gateway Integration support tool designs. These designs are non-functional and are minimally interactive. They are intended to be an additional way for developers to view the designs, allowing for cleaner CSS styles than what Figma can output.

Pages

These are links to the various pages of the tool. Because the pages are non-functional, this is the best way to find the page you are looking for. These pages will each open in a new tab.
